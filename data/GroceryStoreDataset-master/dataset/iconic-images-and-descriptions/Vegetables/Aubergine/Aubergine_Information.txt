Title: Aubergine Class 1 
Country: Spain 
Description: Aubergine, or eggplant as it is also called, is usually dark violet. It has a shiny peel and is 15-20 cm long. The pulp is white. Aubergine is always eaten cooked. In addition to Moussaka, Aubergine fits well in pots and gratins.
